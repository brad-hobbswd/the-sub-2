## About the Book
The Substitute explores a question that reaches beyond addiction itself and into the deeper realities of the human heart:
**What is happening inside the heart of the person who needs the substitute?**
While many conversations about addiction focus on substances, behaviors, consequences, and recovery, this book examines the emotional, relational, and spiritual realities that often exist beneath the struggle. Through discussions of emotional wounds, shame, fear, loneliness, identity, recovery, healing, and faith, readers are invited to look beyond the visible behavior and discover the deeper story unfolding within the human heart.
This second volume continues the journey begun in *What Is the Addiction Substituting For?* while exploring how pain, unmet needs, and the search for relief often shape a person's experience long before addiction becomes visible.
